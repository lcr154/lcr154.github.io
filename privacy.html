Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Content Bot ("we", "our", "the app") is a content management tool that helps users create and publish content across social media platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Content Bot, we may collect:

  • Instagram account information (username, account ID) necessary for publishing content
  • Content you create through the app (text, images)
  • Publishing history and statistics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for:

  • Publishing content to your connected social media accounts on your behalf
  • Displaying publishing history and statistics within the app
  • Improving app functionality

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. API tokens are stored encrypted and used only for authorized publishing actions.

5. Third-Party Services

Content Bot integrates with the following services for content publishing:

  • Instagram (Meta Platforms) - for publishing posts
  • Telegram - for publishing to channels
  • VK - for publishing to communities
  • Yandex Zen - for publishing articles via RSS

Each service has its own privacy policy that governs the use of data on their platforms.

6. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time. See our Data Deletion Instructions for details.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke app permissions at any time through your Instagram settings

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us through the Telegram bot interface or create an issue in our GitHub repository.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
