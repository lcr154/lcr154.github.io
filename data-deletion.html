Data Deletion Instructions

How to delete your data

If you want to delete all data associated with your account in Content Bot, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Content Bot in Telegram
  2. Send the command /settings
  3. Select "Delete my data"
  4. Confirm the deletion

What gets deleted

  • Your account settings and API tokens
  • Publishing history
  • Generated content and images
  • All associated metadata

Revoking Instagram access

To revoke Content Bot's access to your Instagram account:

  1. Open Instagram
  2. Go to Settings -> Apps and Websites
  3. Find "Content Bot" in the Active tab
  4. Tap "Remove"

Timeline

Data deletion is processed immediately upon request. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 24 hours.

Contact

If you have any issues with data deletion, please contact us through the Telegram bot or create an issue in our GitHub repository.
